The Complete Framework

7 Steps to Healthy Skin

1
Confirm Malassezia on cytology — do not treat blind
2
Treat with evidence-based topicals (miconazole-CHG shampoo first-line) + adjuncts
3
Investigate the underlying cause — atopy is #1 in bulldogs
4
Manage itch to break the scratch-infection cycle (Cytopoint is the community game-changer)
5
Maintain with daily fold care, weekly baths, environmental optimization
6
Consider immunotherapy for long-term resolution — commitment required, transformative results
7
Never stop asking why. The yeast is the symptom, not the disease.
